About Us

We are a community-driven organization focused on fighting climate change through awareness, education, and hands-on projects. Our goal is to build a sustainable future for all generations.

Our Projects

Tree Planting Initiative

Our reforestation projects will help restore natural habitats and combat global warming.

Community Cleanups

We organize regular cleanups to remove waste from sacred ghats.

Recycle

Using the waste accumulated from ghats and recycling it to fund our goals and support other NGOs.

Collaborations

We welcome volunteers, donations, and partnerships. Join us in making a difference!

We collaborate with multiple institutions near sacred ghats and with different schools, colleges, and universities to volunteer for saving our ghats and fight deforestation.
